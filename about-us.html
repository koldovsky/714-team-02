About Cilantro

Cilantro Travel has passed a long way since its early beginnings in 1988. The company was founded by two ordinary guys passionate about tourism. At the moment, the company serves more than 15,000 travellers across Europe each year.

Our professional team has been lucky to travel to a number of amazing countries across all continents and can proudly boast many years of practical expertise in the travel industry. They are committed to excellence in providing clients with high-quality travel and the best possible traveling experience at reasonable prices. Due to the time spent abroad, they clearly understand all the demands of a modern traveler. Our specialists are always friendly and pay individual attention to each client. Expert guides will happily share with you all of their favourite spots.

Out mission and vision

Cilantro Travel has come a long way since its humble beginnings in 1997. A company that began with two bearded backpackers, a typewriter and a kitchen table now leads 10,000 travelers across the Europe each year. Our team have been lucky enough to travel to a huge variety of incredible countries across all seven continents, and collectively boast many years of experience in the travel industry to compliment our modern approach to providing quality travel.
Because of our time spent abroad, we know exactly what the modern traveller wants from a destination specialist. Our specialists offer an honest, informed approach to planning your holiday and we return regularly to our offered regions to check on any new products and destinations and to conduct a quality check of the products we already know and love.
